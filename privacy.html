PRIVACY POLICY – HABIT HERO

Last Updated: June 10, 2026

Habit Hero respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information.

Habit Hero is a habit tracking and productivity application designed to help users build positive routines, monitor progress, and achieve long-term goals.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Habit Hero operates primarily using data stored locally on your device.

The app may store:

  • Habit names and categories
  • Habit schedules and reminders
  • Progress statistics and streak information
  • Achievement and badge progress
  • Theme and preference settings

This information remains on your device unless future cloud backup features are explicitly enabled by you.

2. PERSONAL DATA

Habit Hero does not require account creation.

We do not collect:

  • Names
  • Phone numbers
  • Government IDs
  • Contacts
  • Photos
  • Location data
  • Messages

3. ADVERTISING

Habit Hero may display advertisements through Google AdMob.

Google and its advertising partners may collect limited anonymous advertising identifiers for ad delivery, fraud prevention, and performance measurement.

For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. NOTIFICATIONS

Habit Hero may request notification permission to deliver habit reminders and scheduled alerts.

These notifications are generated on your device and are not used to collect personal information.

5. DATA STORAGE

All habit records, achievements, statistics, and settings are stored locally on your device.

Deleting the application or clearing application data may permanently remove your stored progress.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Habit Hero is not directed toward children under the age required by local laws for independent consent.

7. SECURITY

Reasonable security measures are implemented to protect locally stored data. However, no electronic system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

8. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES

  • Google AdMob (Advertising)
  • Google Play Services

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

9. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically.

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this document will reflect the latest revision.
